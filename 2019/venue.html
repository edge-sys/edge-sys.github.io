Venue

The workshop will be held in Room 0540, which is located in the main building of Technical University of Munich at Arcisstrasse 21. The venue is on the downtown campus of TUM in the center of Munich. The main building has been the administrative and management centre of the university since 1868. The venue is easily reachable in the city center and trams run nearby.
